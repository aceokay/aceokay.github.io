Craigslist Scraper

A Ruby web app to scrape Craigslist's Missed Connections

Built within a team of four, our custom scraper can take user-generated queries and have all relevant posts returned to a user's profile for future reference. All from live Craigslist postings.

  • Scraper Build: Combination of the gems Mechanize and Nokogiri
  • Back-end: Sinatra, PG, and ActiveRecord

Hiresly

An Ember-backed web app built to connect employers with capable developer talent.

Still in development, this is meant to allow Devs of all skills to impress employers through coding challenges. A reverse job board that grants employers the opportunity to screen applicants online with real code.

  • Back-end: Ember and Firebase
