GYR (Green|Yellow|Red)

HackPDX 2015 1st place project

A web suite for teachers to manage student records while keeping parents fully informed.

Teachers can track behavior, make individual comments on students, mark absences/tardies, detail the day's lessons, detail and attached the evening's homework, and finally email parents all that information from one screen. The live demo was made within 10 hours.

  • Build: Ruby on Rails and PostgreSQL
  • Teammates: Aileen Cacayorin | Shannon Mulloy | Caterina Paun

Craigslist Scraper
