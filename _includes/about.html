My Story

I am a newly minted Software Developer with a love for all things built in the back-end. I found my love for programming in the Philippines as a Peace Corps Volunteer and I've brought that passion home and through all my projects since. Today I'm working as an intern with Hint in Portland and am working with a team crushing bugs and building superior products.

As to where I came from, here's a quick breakdown of my personal history.

Pre-Programming

Grew up living on a boat, moved to a cabin without running water or electricity, and finally joined the Peace Corps after earning my degree from Western Washington University. The typical origin story.

Programming Revelation

Encountered a web marketing project as a Peace Corps Volunteer and finally dipped my toe into programming. This initiated a chain of learning, culminating in quitting my job in market research to go full-time as a student at Epicodus, making the jump to the Developer world.

Full-Tilt Programming

Over the course of my time at Epicodus, I created applications in Ruby, Javascript, and used frameworks such as Rails and Ember. I've learned that given enough effort and dedication, I can master any programming language. Bring it on.
